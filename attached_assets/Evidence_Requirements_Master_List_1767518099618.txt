Evidence Requirements Master List
1. IDENTIFICATION
Photographic ID
Driver's Licence

Generic: A copy of both the back and front of your current Driver's Licence.

P&N Bank Requirement: Requires both Driver's Licence and Passport to satisfy VOI/AML policies (must submit both).

Passport

Generic: A copy of the photographic page of your current passport. If expired, it must not be more than 2 years expired.

P&N Bank Requirement: Requires both Driver's Licence and Passport.

Non-Photographic ID
Medicare Card: A copy of your current Medicare Card.

Birth Certificate: A copy of your birth certificate.

Marriage Certificate: A copy of your marriage certificate.

Citizenship Certificate: A copy of your citizenship certificate.

Permanent Residency: A copy of your permanent residency documentation.

2. INCOME
PAYG (Pay As You Go)
Payslip (Standard)

Generic: The latest two consecutive payslips.

Payslip (Lender Specific)

ABL: Requires two of the following: Current payslip (<60 days old, with YTD), Employer letter (<60 days old), Current FY Income Statement (<45 days old), or 'Tax ready' Income Statement from the most recent FY.

ANZ: The most recent payslip AND the last 3 months' bank statements showing receipt of income.

GSB: The most recent payslip AND the last 1 monthâ€™s bank statement showing receipt of income.

Self Employed
Individual Tax Return {ITR}: Copies for the latest 2 financial years.

Notice of Assessment {NOA}: Copies from the ATO for the latest 2 financial years.

Company Tax Return {CTR}: Copies for the latest 2 financial years.

Company Financial Statements {CFS}: Copies for the latest 2 financial years.

Trust Tax Return {TTR}: Copies for the latest 2 financial years.

Trust Financial Statements {TFS}: Copies for the latest 2 financial years.

BAS Statements: Copies of the latest 6 months' worth of BAS statements.

Accountant's Letter: A letter from your accountant (typically required if other docs are unavailable).

Rental Income
Existing Rental Income: Evidence can include an unexpired lease agreement, the most recent monthly management statement, a full valuation (if used as security), bank statements showing rental receipt history, or a tax return.

Proposed Rental Income: A letter from a real estate agent stating market rental value, or a valuation report.

Government & Other Income
Centrelink: The latest issued/available Centrelink Income Statement (must be the official PDF, not a screenshot).

Child Support (Non-Centrelink): An official document (Court Order, Private Agreement/BFA, or Statutory Declaration) AND 6 months' bank statements showing consistent receipt.

3. ASSETS
Genuine Savings: Evidence of 5% of the purchase price accumulated or held for at least 3 months (e.g., 3 months of bank statements). Shares held for 3 months also count.

Funds to Complete: Evidence you control the funds for the transaction (Bank statement, Contract of Sale for a house sale, Consent Order for payouts, or Stat Dec for gifts).

Gifted Funds: A fully executed Statutory Declaration from the donor stating the funds are a gift and do not need to be repaid.

4. LIABILITIES (Ongoing Debts)
Debts you intend to keep after settlement.

Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL): Latest statement showing name, account number, institution, and running balance.

Credit Card: Latest credit card statement (unless lender uses CCR).

Personal Loan: Latest personal loan statement.

Home Loan: Latest home loan statement.

HELP Debt: Latest HELP debt statement from the ATO (via MyGov).

5. REFINANCE (Debts being paid out)
Debts you are refinancing with the new loan.

Credit Cards: Statements for the 3 most recent months.

Personal Loans: Statements for the 3 most recent months.

Home Loans: Statements for the 6 most recent months.

6. PURCHASE & PROPERTY OWNERSHIP
Purchase Documents
Contract of Sale: A copy of the "fully executed" contract of sale including all annexures.

Deposit Receipt: Receipt proving the Real Estate agent has received the deposit funds.

First Home Owners Grant (FHOG): A copy of the "fully executed" FHOG form (original may be required later).

Property Ownership (Existing Property)
Rates Notice:

Generic: A copy of your latest rates statement.

ING / St George: Explicitly require a rates notice to evidence ownership if the property is being used as security.

7. CONSTRUCTION
Building Contract: A copy of the Fixed Price building contract, including the progress payment schedule, plans, specifications, and any variations.

Additional Works: Copies of invoices or quotes for any additional works not included in the fixed price contract.

8. EXPENSES
Declared Living Expenses: An estimate of your living expenses as they are expected to be at settlement.
