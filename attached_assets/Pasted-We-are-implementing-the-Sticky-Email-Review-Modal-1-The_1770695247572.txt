2. The Logic (public/js/marketing.js)

State: Create a global currentReviewEmail and currentReviewName.

Constants: Define DEFAULT_FOLLOWUP_BODY with {{NAME}} placeholders.

Functions:

openEmailReview(email, name):

Store email and name.

Retrieve the saved template from localStorage.getItem('integrity_email_template').

If none exists, use DEFAULT_FOLLOWUP_BODY.

Populate #reviewBody with that template (showing {{NAME}}).

Populate #reviewSubject.

Show the modal.

executeGmailHandoff():

Step 1 (Save): Get the text from #reviewBody and save it to localStorage.setItem('integrity_email_template', text). This ensures their edits stick for next time.

Step 2 (Personalize): Replace {{NAME}} with currentReviewName.

Step 3 (Clipboard): navigator.clipboard.writeText(personalizedText).

Step 4 (Open): Open Gmail URL (view=cm, to, su ONLY).

Step 5 (Feedback): Show a toast: "Copied! Press Ctrl+V in Gmail."

resetFollowUpTemplate():

Clear localStorage.

Reset #reviewBody to DEFAULT_FOLLOWUP_BODY.

Action: Implement the Sticky Modal. Ensure that clicking 'Copy & Open' updates the stored template so the user doesn't have to re-edit it for the next contact.
